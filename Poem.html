Poems

My oh my,
What a sight to see,
What a thing to behold,
What a smile to see,
Will you let me be with you,
All the days of my life?

I've never been this happy,
I've never been this sad,
I've never felt such strong emotions,
Than when we kissed,
When we went away,
When we got back,
And when we stayed.
It never really struck me--
The things they say are worth living for,
Because to me,
There's no one else I'd rather live my life for,
Than the girl who gave me everything to die for.

I have uttered a lot of words in my life,
Yet this is the first time I yearned to know all the ways to say I love you.
I have learned a lot of things in my life,
Yet this is the first time I wanted to dedicate them to someone like you.
I have loved all of my life,
And yet,
This is the first time I have ever wanted to love someone as hard as you.
Of all the things I love to do,
I would now just like to do them with you.
There's nothing else,
You are complete.

Letters

Dear Princess,
I have always adored you,
From the moment we locked eyes,
To the time we held hands,
From the time I saw your smile,
To the moment I felt your touch.
I love you, more than words can ever say.
You saved me from my misery,
You gave my life more color,
All the things I've done,
All the things I know,
All of the things I have,
I dedicate them all to you.
You are amazing.

Hello! Who would ever thought I would have the chance to do something like this again?
We parted our ways, but we found our way back to each other.
I'm still thankful, for the decision you made.
I vow to always make you the happiest girl in the world, no matter what.
You're still my girl, and you'll forever be my girl.
Mahal na mahal kita.
Hindi ko siguro alam kung nasaan ako ngayon kung wala ka.
Salamat at nariyan ka.
Salamat at mahal mo ako.
Mga pagkakataong kasama kita;
Mga kulitan, tawanan, tinginan,
Lahat sila ang nagbibigay ng buhay sakin.
Hindi ako mapapagod.
They say the bright rays of the sun makes for a golden hour,
But every moment spent with you, is my golden hour.
Every moment laughing with you, is every rain and storm pushed away.
A life without you, wouldn't be something I'd be happy to live.

I know everything, and I appreciate all of them.
The things you do, the things you think of,
The things you want, the things you wish to be,
I see them all, in your eyes and in your mind.
Continue to grow, love, and be the person you've always wished to be.
I'll always be here, and I will always be proud of you.
This is a letter of appreciation especially for you.
Thank you so much for everything.
I wouldn't be here without you.
That's why I always want to be there for you, because I know you need someone besides you.
I won't get tired of listening to you.
I won't get tired of telling you you're beautiful.
I won't get tired of holding your hand.
I won't get tired of you.
My heart is full of love and appreciation to none other than you.
If I could only take it out and give it to you, you'll understand how much I really mean the things I say.
You'll understand what my words really mean.
You'll understand how much I really love you.
If only I can give you everything, I wouldn't mind having nothing.
You're the best person in the world, and no one can tell me otherwise.
You make me happy.
You make me strong.
You make me---ME. I love you so much, Princess Nicole, I offer you my heart.
